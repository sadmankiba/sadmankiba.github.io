Undergrad Student in Computer Science

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology

I am an networking/IoT enthusiast and my research interest lies broadly in:

  • designing architectures and protocols for small to large-scale networking

  • diversifying application of sensing and sensor networks

  • improving performance of wireless technologies

  • building cost efficient and faster DNN architecture by ensuring parallel training

I am currently a 4th year undergrad student in Computer Science Department at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), under the supervision of Dr. M. Shohel Rahman. Currently, I am also working on IoT-VR project with Dr. Matthew Caesar.

I consider myself an exploratory researcher and I am deeply interested in networking, Internet of Things, mobile and ubiquitous computing, wireless networks and embedded systems.
